# Uchronies & Treasures

**A Cairn hack of temporal heists and financial desperation.**

---

Las Vegas, 2080.

You work for the Temporal Division.
Pay: $800 a week. Expenses: $900 a week.
The math does not add up.

**Madame Zhou is waiting.**

---

*"Vegas was never an honest city. But at least before, the rules were clear. Now? Time itself is rigged."*

---

A game about desperate people in impossible situations. About the slow math of debt and the faster math of corruption. About the permanent cost of every choice you make.

**Requires:** 2-5 players, one Warden, polyhedral dice, and a willingness to ask hard questions.

- 15 minutes to create a character.
- One session to feel the pressure.
- Ten sessions to see who you have become.
