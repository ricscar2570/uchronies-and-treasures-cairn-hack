# Character Creation Checklist

*Quick reference for creating a PC in 15 minutes. For full details: Character Creation chapter.*

---

## 1. Ability Scores (1 min)

Roll **3d6 three times**, in order: **STR, DEX, WIL.** You may swap any two results.

| Attribute | Used for |
|-----------|----------|
| **STR** | Physical power, endurance, resisting poison |
| **DEX** | Agility, reflexes, stealth, shooting |
| **WIL** | Mental resilience, perception, social influence, resisting contamination |

## 2. Hit Protection (30 sec)

Roll **1d6.** This is your HP. It reflects luck and reflexes, not toughness.

## 3. Background (2 min)

Roll **d6** or choose:

| d6 | Background | Gear Package |
|----|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | **Ex-Military** | Combat knife (d6), compass, Zippo lighter |
| 2 | **Failed Academic** | 3 rare books (bulky), magnifying lens, notebook |
| 3 | **Reformed Criminal** | Lockpick set, 1 criminal contact, burner phone |
| 4 | **Corrupt Cop** | Fake detective badge, handcuffs, pepper spray (d4) |
| 5 | **Disbarred Medic** | Field med kit (5 uses, d4 STR), morphine (3 vials), scalpel (d6) |
| 6 | **Con Artist** | 3 fake IDs, $500 extra cash, fake Rolex |

## 4. Standard Equipment

All agents start with:

- Glock 17 pistol (d6, 1 slot)
- 2 extra magazines (1 slot)
- Basic first aid kit (3 uses, d4 STR, 1 slot)
- Anti-rejection medicine, 4 weeks (1 slot)
- 3 days rations (1 slot)
- Light tactical vest, kevlar II (1 Armor, worn)
- Temporal protection suit (reduces contamination WIL damage by 1, worn)
- Encrypted cellphone, Division badge, $500 cash (pockets, 0 slots)

**Total slots used:** 5 standard + background items. **10 slots maximum.**

## 5. Trackers

| Tracker | Start |
|---------|-------|
| Loyalty | 5 |
| Corruption | 0 |
| Certificates | 0 |
| Debt | -$500 |
| Temporal Quirks | 0/5 |
| Tier | Recruit ($800/wk) |

**Instability:** triggers when |Loyalty - Corruption| is 2 or less.

**Medicine:** $250/week, mandatory. Missing a dose = Deprived + WIL degrades 1/week.

## 6. Traits (2 min)

Roll d10 on each: Physique, Face, Speech, Vice, Virtue (see Character Creation chapter).

## 7. Narrative Details (3 min)

Answer in 1-2 sentences each:

- **Why did you join the Division?**
- **What debt or problem keeps you desperate?**
- **One person you care about (alive or dead)?**

---

*Total time: ~15 minutes. The character is ready to play.*
