## Exploration & Time

### Time Structure

The game uses three timescales:

**Rounds** (10 seconds): Used during combat and tense action sequences. A character can move 40 feet and take one action per round.

**Turns** (10 minutes): Used during exploration of temporal zones. Searching a room, picking a lock, administering first aid, or carefully moving through a corridor all take one turn.

**Weeks**: The fundamental unit of campaign time. Each week consists of a mission phase (1-3 days in a temporal zone), downtime (rest, side gigs, personal business), and administrative time (pay, expenses, tracker updates).

### Exploration Procedure

When the party enters a temporal zone, the Warden describes the environment. Players declare their actions. The Warden determines results based on fiction, calling for saves only when the outcome is uncertain and the stakes are meaningful.

**Every 3 turns (30 minutes of exploration)**, the Warden rolls on the encounter table or advances the zone's clock. Temporal zones are active: anomalies shift, echoes patrol, scavengers hunt.

**Generic Zone Encounter Table (d6):**

| d6 | Encounter |
|----|-----------|
| 1 | **Quiet.** Tension builds but nothing happens. Describe an unsettling sensory detail. |
| 2 | **Environmental hazard.** Structural collapse, gravity anomaly, or temporal rift. DEX or WIL save to avoid. |
| 3 | **Signs of passage.** Footprints, discarded equipment, blood trail. Someone else is here. |
| 4 | **Temporal echo** (hostile). 4 HP, 8 STR, 12 DEX, 6 WIL, temporal claw (d6). |
| 5 | **Scavengers** (d4). Reaction roll to determine attitude. Armed with knives (d6) and pistols (d6). |
| 6 | **Temporal event.** Time stutters. Everyone makes a WIL save or loses their next turn (frozen in a loop). |

**Every 6 hours of zone exposure**, each character makes a WIL save against contamination (see the Contamination chapter).

### Light and Darkness

Temporal zones often have unpredictable lighting. Carry torches or flashlights (3 hours battery, 1 slot). Working in darkness without light means all actions are **impaired**.

### Hazards

**Temporal rifts:** Patches of unstable time. Walking through one may teleport you elsewhere in the zone, forward or backward in time, or damage your gear. DEX save to avoid, or WIL save to navigate intentionally.

**Echo patrols:** Former agents who became temporal echoes. Hostile and unpredictable.

**Scavengers:** Unaffiliated humans who enter zones illegally to loot. Unpredictable. Reaction roll determines their attitude.

**Structural collapse:** Buildings in temporal zones decay at different rates. Floors may give way, walls may crumble. DEX save to react.

### Rest in the Zone

Resting in a temporal zone restores HP but does NOT stop the contamination clock. Every 6 hours of presence counts, whether you're moving or resting.

Safe rooms (rare, marked on Division maps) reduce contamination checks to every 12 hours.
